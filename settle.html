uGigUs Quick Start Guide
🎟️ Revenues 📊 Stats 💸 Expenses 🎤 Earnings ➕ Additions ➖ Deductions

🎟️ Ticket Revenues

  • Click the plus icon and match each line to the audit.

📊 Ticket Stats - Drop Count

  • Enter sold, comps, and drop count.

💸 Expenses

  • Log each cost and assign who incurs it (promoter, venue, etc.).

🎤 Artist Earnings

  • After all of the show revenues and expenses are entered into the system, uGigUs calculates total artist earnings. Enter the actual artist earnings.

➕ Artist Additions: Buyouts & Reimbursements

  • Plug in meal buyouts, reimbursements, and any extras the artist should be paid for.

➖ Payout Deductions: Deposits and Past Payments

  • Account for any deposits or wires already paid — balance due to artist adjusts automatically.
