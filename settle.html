🎟️ Ticket Revenues

  • Enter revenue lines shown on the ticketing audit.
  • Use proper tax settings based on whether fees are included.
  • Click the blue plus icon to add more entries.

📊 Ticket Stats - Drop Count

  • Enter total sold and comp counts here.
  • Drop count = sold + comp (used for tax/proration).
  • Ensure numbers align with the box office audit.

💸 Expenses

  • Enter each expense with its value and notes.
  • Use red dots to flag who is incurring the cost.
  • Use the “+” to add additional line items.

🎤 Artist Earnings

  • Guarantee is auto-pulled from the deal sheet.
  • Use overage or bonuses if applicable.
  • Balance Due is calculated automatically.

➕ Artist Additions: Buyouts & Reimbursements

  • Enter meal buyouts, gas, or lodging reimbursements here.
  • Attach backup receipts if needed.
  • This increases total artist earnings.

➖ Payout Deductions: Artist Deposits

  • Deduct any advance wires or deposits here.
  • Final payout = Total Earned - Deductions
  • Use the notes field to clarify payment details.
